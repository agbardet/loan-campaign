Business Problem

AllLife Bank wants to grow its personal loan portfolio by converting existing liability (deposit) customers. Only 9.6% of 5,000 customers accepted a previous loan offer — making untargeted outreach expensive and low-conversion.

A propensity model identifies the right customers to contact, reducing cost per acquisition and enabling marketing teams to focus on the highest-likelihood acceptors. The model captures 9 in 10 actual loan acceptors while maintaining 92.9% precision.
